Academics at De La Salle University

De La Salle University offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen fields.

Colleges and Schools

College of Liberal Arts (CLA)

Programs in humanities, social sciences, and communication arts.

College of Science (COS)

Courses in mathematics, biology, chemistry, and physics.

College of Business (COB)

Business administration, marketing, and financial management programs.

College of Engineering (COE)

Engineering programs including mechanical, civil, and electrical engineering.

College of Computer Studies (CCS)

Information technology, computer science, and software engineering programs.

School of Economics (SOE)

Economics, finance, and business economics programs.

Programs Offered

  • Undergraduate Programs: BS, BA, and BFA degrees in various disciplines.
  • Graduate Programs: MA, MS, MBA, and PhD programs.
  • Doctoral Programs: Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) and Doctor of Education (EdD).

Academic Calendar

The academic year at DLSU is divided into three terms:

  • Term 1: August – December
  • Term 2: January – April
  • Term 3: May – July

Seminars, workshops, and conferences are held throughout the year to enhance learning experiences.

Learning Resources

De La Salle University provides students with access to extensive learning resources, including:

  • Modern libraries with digital and physical collections.
  • Online learning platforms and e-resources.
  • State-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities.
