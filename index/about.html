About De La Salle University

Founded in 1911, De La Salle University (DLSU) is a Catholic institution of higher learning in the Philippines renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, research, and community service. Located in Manila, the university offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across various fields.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To be a leading learner-centered research university, bridging faith and scholarship, producing global citizens for God and country.

Vision: Inspired by the charism of Saint John Baptist de La Salle, DLSU is committed to providing human and Christian education to the youth, especially the poor.

Core Values

  • Faith (Religio): Commitment to God and the pursuit of truth.
  • Service (Mores): Ethical behavior and service to society.
  • Communion (Cultura): Fostering inclusivity, respect, and collaboration.

Campus and Facilities

The Manila campus is equipped with modern facilities, including libraries, laboratories, and research centers. The university also has satellite campuses that support specialized programs and courses.
