Student Life at De La Salle University

Experience a vibrant and enriching student life at DLSU. With a variety of student organizations, events, and campus facilities, students have numerous opportunities to learn, grow, and build lifelong memories.

Student Organizations

University Student Government (USG)

The USG represents the student body, organizes events, and advocates for student interests.

DLSU Chorale

A premier choral group showcasing musical talent through performances and competitions.

La Salle Dance Company

A group for passionate dancers performing in various university and external events.

Campus Events and Activities

  • Annual University Fair with performances, food stalls, and games.
  • Leadership Training Seminars and workshops.
  • Intramural Sports and intercollegiate competitions.
  • Cultural events celebrating Filipino heritage and diversity.

Campus Facilities

Our campus provides state-of-the-art facilities that enhance student experiences, including:

  • Fully-equipped libraries with study areas and research resources.
  • Sports complex featuring a gym, pool, and sports courts.
  • Student centers with lounges and meeting rooms.
  • On-campus dining options offering a variety of cuisines.

Student Support Services

We prioritize student well-being by offering:

  • Counseling and mental health services.
  • Career development and job placement assistance.
  • Health services and medical clinics on campus.
  • Academic support and tutoring programs.
