Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completed application form.
  • Official high school transcript of records.
  • Certificate of good moral character.
  • Birth certificate (NSO/PSA certified).
  • Two recent 2x2 ID photos.

Graduate Programs

  • Completed application form.
  • Transcript of records from previous college/university.
  • Two letters of recommendation.
  • Updated curriculum vitae (CV).
  • Personal statement or essay.

Application Process

  1. Online Application: Complete the online application form available on the DLSU admissions portal.
  2. Submit Requirements: Upload scanned copies of required documents.
  3. Pay Application Fee: Payment can be made online or at the admissions office.
  4. Entrance Examination: Take the DLSU College Admission Test (DCAT) for undergraduate applicants.
  5. Interview: Some programs may require an interview.
  6. Admission Decision: You will receive an acceptance or rejection notification via email.

Scholarships and Financial Aid

De La Salle University offers a variety of scholarships and financial aid programs, including:

  • Academic Scholarships: Full and partial scholarships for students with outstanding academic performance.
  • Athletic Scholarships: For students who excel in sports.
  • Need-based Financial Aid: Assistance for students with financial challenges.
  • Government and Private Grants: Available through partnerships with external organizations.

International Students

DLSU welcomes international students. Additional requirements include:

  • Valid passport and visa.
  • Proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL).
  • Clearance from the Bureau of Immigration.
