Welcome to De La Salle University

Your gateway to excellence in education, research, and service.

About Us

De La Salle University (DLSU) is a leading private Catholic research university in the Philippines. Founded in 1911 by the Brothers of the Christian Schools, DLSU provides education rooted in the values of faith, service, and excellence. DLSU has a rich history of producing leaders who have shaped the Philippines’ development and made an impact globally.

Our mission is to produce graduates who are highly skilled, socially responsible, and have the ability to make a positive impact in society. Through innovative research, quality education, and a strong sense of community, we prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Academics

De La Salle University offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and post-graduate programs. Our academic programs emphasize critical thinking, leadership, and the development of technical and soft skills.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Business Administration (Marketing, Management, Accounting)
  • Engineering (Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Chemical)
  • Computer Science (AI, Data Science, Software Engineering)
  • Social Sciences (Political Science, Psychology, Sociology)

Graduate Programs

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Science in Engineering
  • PhD in Management

Admissions

At De La Salle University, we welcome students who are eager to grow academically and personally. Our admission process is designed to evaluate candidates holistically, considering both academic performance and other criteria such as extracurricular activities and leadership potential.

How to Apply

To apply, students must complete an online application and submit their academic records, recommendation letters, and a personal essay. DLSU also holds entrance exams for certain programs.

Research

De La Salle University is committed to advancing knowledge through research and innovation. Our research programs focus on addressing real-world problems, and we collaborate with industries and communities to produce research that impacts society positively.

Our faculty members are involved in a variety of research projects, and students are encouraged to participate in research activities as part of their academic journey.
